About Us

Welcome to The LovelynClair Foundation

The LovelynClair Foundation is a nonprofit organisation committed to building society and touching lives.

We do this by working with and empowering society's most vulnerable, widows who have been disinherited or at risk of harmful cultural practices; the aged that have been abandoned by their families and the society, restive youths and underserved teenagers at risk of delinquency and orphans without a support network to help them achieve their dreams.

The LovelynClair foundation has carried out economic empowerment and support outreaches to old peoples homes, orphanages, disabled peoples homes and underserved teenagers in the inner cities of our communities, in furtherance of her mission.

Our Believe

We believe that when society lends a hand to those who have become vulnerable by reasons of lack of education, disability, old age and other factors, we are invariably improving their lives and life outcomes.

The LovelynClair Foundation through the platform of her mentoring programm 'THE LOVELYNCLAIR MENTORING ACADEMY, mentors young people both in academics and skills. 

We empower young people after been mentored in our program for a better life so as not to constitute nuisance to the society.

We encourage multi-skaholder involvements in all our interventions.

We carry out quaterly distributions of products, cash and render services to the above mentioned groups which we select quarterly.
